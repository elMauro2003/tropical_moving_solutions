Meet Our Founder

Antony Días

Founded by Anthony Diaz, a passionate entrepreneur with a sharp eye for business and a deep desire to help people, Tropical Moving Solutions was born from a simple idea: moving should be easier. Anthony saw a need for a service that put people first — with friendly movers, honest pricing, and reliable scheduling — and he built a company that reflects those values every day.
